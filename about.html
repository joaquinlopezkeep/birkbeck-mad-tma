About Us

Welcome to ZeDorm, your number one source for all things student accommodation. We're dedicated to providing you with the very best of accommodation, with an emphasis on price, proximity and comfort.

Founded in 2020 by ZedLand University's DCS, ZeDorm has come a long way from its beginnings in the DCS labs. When DCS first started out, their passion for web apps drove them to start their own app.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
